What We Treat

Specialized Services

Comprehensive orthopedic care using the most advanced surgical techniques available today.

01
Joint Replacement

Total knee & hip replacement using cutting-edge prosthetics for permanent pain relief and full mobility restoration.

02
Sports Injuries

Expert management of ACL tears, fractures, dislocations & overuse injuries in athletes and active individuals.

03
Spine Disorders

Treatment of herniated discs, spinal stenosis & chronic back pain through surgical and non-surgical methods.

04
Arthroscopy

Minimally invasive keyhole surgery for diagnosing and treating joint problems in the knee, shoulder, hip & ankle.

05
Fracture Management

State-of-the-art fixation using plates, nails & external fixators for optimal healing and recovery outcomes.

06
Physiotherapy & Rehab

Customized rehabilitation programs restoring strength, flexibility and function after surgery or injury.
